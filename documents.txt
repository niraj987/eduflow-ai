Project Overview

Imagine a platform like a personalized version of Coursera, Khan Academy, and ChatGPT combined.

The system:

Creates a personalized learning path.
Tracks student performance.
Predicts future scores.
Recommends topics to study.
Generates notes, quizzes, and explanations automatically.
Answers questions from textbooks using RAG.
